ABOUT SHOP FOR GOOD

Who are we?

Shop for Good is an organisation that promotes giving to charity through allowing users to purchasing various items and giving the profits to charities

Our mission
Help give to charity

When you use our website, you help better and strengthen our society and our community

We believe that when you offer a helping hand to others, you create opportunities to meet new people who believe in the same causes that inspire you

Our intention is to cause a chain reaction of helpfulness

The Process
What are the steps?

Step one

We choose the appropriate items to sell to our clients.

Step two

You purchase the item online, via our website.

Step three

Your money goes towards a charity!
